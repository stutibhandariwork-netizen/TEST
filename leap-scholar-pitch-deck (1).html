Pillar 1

Engagement Strategy

  • Weekly themed discussions — Visa tips, scholarship stories, destination spotlights
  • Parent AMAs — Families whose kids study abroad share their real journey
  • Expert office hours — Counselors, visa officers, finance advisors go live weekly
  • Milestone posts — "Got my I-20!", "Visa approved!" celebrated in the community
  • Gamified participation — Badges, leaderboards, monthly top parent recognition
Daily Active Habit
Something new every day — a question, a story, a live session. Parents return because there's always signal, never noise.
Editorial Content Calendar
Structured weekly themes ensure the forum never goes cold. Content planning built in from day one.
UGC Loop
Prompt parents to share their journey. One parent's post becomes another's inspiration. Community feeds itself.
